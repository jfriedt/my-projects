Genius Hour

UC Davis Home

Over these past few weeks, for my Genius Hour I've attended multiple seminars on computer science and engineering. Women from UC Davis, California have held zooms exclusively for people who identify as women and want to learn more about careers in engineering. I've learned how much each career makes, how to find help within the college campus and programs, and what exactly each job needs and what they look for. This experience has taught me a lot and has mademy love for computer science and engineering grow!
